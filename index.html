Fungible Tokens

With this simple DAPP you can mint your fungible tokens without sharing your private key or seed. You will need the TON wallet extension for Chrome.
The cost of one mint of a fungible token is 2.5 TON (+ 0.1 TON for blockchain fees).
You can manage your tokens (or any other tokens) through our DeFiWallet.
In the next future, you will be able to trade your tokens on the Tonox Dex.

Non-Fungible Tokens

With this simple DAPP you can mint your non-fungible tokens without sharing your private key or seed. You will need the TON wallet extension for Chrome.
The cost of one mint of a collection of NTFs 1 TON (+ 1 TON for blockchain fees).
The cost of one mint of an NTF item 0.3 TON (+ 0.05 TON for blockchain fees).
You can manage your NFT through our DeFiWallet.
